Practical Drawing Help

Clean sheets, organized drawings, and production support that helps projects move forward.

I’m Jon Peterson, an experienced architectural draftsman based in Hugoton, Kansas and serving clients across the USA. I produce clean, practical AutoCAD drawings for contractors, homeowners, architects, engineers, property managers, churches, farms, and small businesses. My focus is simple: readable plans, organized sheets, and drawings that help projects move forward.

  • Software: AutoCAD LT
  • 3D visualization: Google SketchUp Online renderings for buildings and project communication.
  • Education: Degree in Architectural Engineering, University of Kansas. Confirm exact degree wording before publishing.
  • Experience: Residential and commercial building drafting support, construction document production, as-builts, redlines, and field-measured drawings.

How It Works

A simple path from rough information to usable drawings.

1

Send project info

Send sketches, photos, PDFs, redlines, field dimensions, or a short project description.

2

Scope review

Jon reviews the information and provides a fixed-fee quote or hourly estimate.

3

Approval

Work begins after scope approval and any agreed deposit.

4

Review and files

Receive review PDFs first, then final PDFs/CAD files as agreed.

Quotes

Fixed-fee quotes after scope review.

Small redline and cleanup jobs can often be quoted quickly. Rush availability may vary by project size and current workload.

Information that helps

  • Project type and location
  • Existing drawings, sketches, photos, or PDFs
  • Needed deliverables and deadline
  • Whether 3D SketchUp rendering is needed
  • Whether a licensed architect or engineer is involved
  • Preferred file types: PDF, DWG, DXF, JPG, PNG, marked-up PDFs

Professional note

Media+Architecture provides CAD drafting, as-built drawings, permit drawing support, redline cleanup, 3D rendering, and construction document production assistance.
